Problems Oleffi solves for MSMEs

Many MSMEs still depend on notebooks, WhatsApp updates, and Excel files that are not connected to the actual production shift. Oleffi creates a simple discipline layer on top of daily manufacturing.

No real production visibility Managers know dispatch pressure, but not the exact machine-wise output and shift loss.
Downtime is not classified properly Electrical, mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, setup, and material losses are mixed together.
ERP does not get reliable shop-floor data Production quantity is captured late, so material consumption and costing become weak.
Quality records are scattered Defect types, pass rate, and lot-wise quality information are difficult to review quickly.

Core product features

Oleffi is designed as a practical SaaS layer for manufacturing discipline. It focuses on what MSMEs can actually adopt on the shop floor.

A

Admin control

Register company once, create machine master, upload product master, add users, disable old users, and manage company code securely.

P

Production capture

Record machine, part number, part name, cycle time, planned quantity, actual quantity, rejection, downtime, and calculated KPIs.

Q

Quality module

Track inspection, defect type, defect quantity, pass rate, and status. The quality module can also be hidden for plants that only want admin plus production.

M

Machine-wise product mapping

Only relevant parts appear for the selected machine. This reduces entry error and improves daily accuracy.

R

Reports and exports

Generate records and export ERP-ready Excel or JSON files for production, quality, downtime, and master data.

S

Simple SaaS rollout

Start in one unit or one department first. Expand after the team becomes comfortable with daily use.

How Oleffi fits into factory workflow

The product is built around a clean sequence: configure once, capture every shift, then export data for review or ERP use.

Factory-side flow

1
Admin sets up company

Machines, product master, users, company code, and module access are configured once.

2
Production team records shift data

Operator selects machine and part, enters quantities, and logs rejection plus downtime.

3
Quality team records inspection

Inspectors capture defect type, defect quantity, pass rate, and quality status.

4
Management reviews performance

Use OEE, downtime, rejection, and machine-wise records for daily review.

Business-side result

1
Faster decision making

Daily production, loss, and rejection visibility improves response speed.

2
Cleaner ERP linkage

Part-wise quantity exported from Oleffi can be used for material consumption, costing, and planning in ERP.

3
Operational discipline

Role-based workflows reduce dependency on verbal updates and disconnected files.

4
Scalable digitalization

MSMEs can begin with the essential modules and add more later without changing the whole process.

Common buyer questions

Can Oleffi work with our existing ERP?

Yes. Oleffi can export production, quality, downtime, and product master data in ERP-friendly formats so your existing ERP team can map and consume the data.

Can we use only Admin and Production?

Yes. The quality module can be hidden for units that want only admin and production workflows.

Is Oleffi suitable for MSMEs?

That is the main target. The product is built to stay practical, simple to train, and easier to adopt than heavy enterprise systems.
